Question
--------

If pwd displays:

$ pwd
/Users/james/thesis

and our files are organised like below, what will 'ls -F backups' display?

Directories
-----------

/Users/james/
	backups/
		original.txt
		final.txt
	thesis/
		current.txt

Answers
-------

A) backups: No such file or directory
B) backups/ thesis/
C) final.txt original.txt
D) current.txt
