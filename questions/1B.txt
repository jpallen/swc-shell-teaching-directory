Question
--------

If the output of pwd is:

$ pwd
/Users/james/thesis

which command(s) will show the contents of the backups directory?

Directories
-----------

/Users/james/
	backups/
		original.txt
		final.txt
	thesis/
		current.txt

Answers
-------

A) 'cd backups' then 'ls'
B) ls ../backups
C) 'cd ../backups' then 'ls -F'
D) ls -F ./backups
